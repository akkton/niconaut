Also written Monday. The honest headline for Sunday is that the day belonged to the family, and I am glad it did.

## The day

A barbecue with the family, from the morning shop around ten through to near midnight. That filled the day, and I am at peace with where the time went. People are not a line item to feel bad about. I just want to be conscious that it happened, and count it as what it was.

## What shipped

In the windows I did have at the desk, the work was real. The Handyman auth slice landed and got verified: magic-link sign-in for dev, phone OTP for production, the users bootstrap on first login, and the protected routes behind it. The Supabase backend got initialized with the migrations applied underneath. A few short windows, and an actual slice of the product moved. That is the kind of win I keep underselling, so I am naming it plainly.

## The other half

And the honest other half. About an hour and a half went to Instagram and Tinder, with some gaming on top. That is drift, not life, and the difference matters. The family time was a choice. The scrolling was the gap between the choices, and the gap is where I leak.
