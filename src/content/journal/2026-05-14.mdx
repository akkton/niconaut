# Day in Brazil 1

First real day at the new desk. Plan was simple: settle in, reactivate Rize, ship some work.

Rize had been off for around two months. I'd told myself I'd come back to it eventually, the way you tell yourself a lot of things. Sitting down this morning, I realized I had no idea what my work weeks actually looked like in terms of hours, focus, where attention was going. Reactivating Rize was the first action of the day, on instinct. Within an hour it was producing data. Within a week, that same data became a feature of this blog. Decisions that compound start small.

The rest of the day was the slow tax of arriving somewhere new. Where the desk goes. Where the wifi is reliable. Where coffee lives. None of that shows up in the Rize categories, but all of it eats focus.

Family on WhatsApp. Brazil is family.
